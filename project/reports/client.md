Client - Micah Bradford - 4/2
    
    As client, I provided a detailed site plan for a fictitious deep-sea salvage company.  I chose two "salvages" to feature and picked out images to represent them.
    I wrote text to match the theme and to fill out the content of the site.  It was a very good site plan.  I also made all of the images availabe for the dev team to access and use.

    Communication with the lead dev was done via email.  To my knowledge, we had no major issues communicating with each other.

    At the time this is written, not every page of the site is complete.  The one page that is finished (the "Black Pearl" page) does appear to very closely match the site plan that I provided, so it is satisfactory.

    If I were to hire a team to make a site for me again, I would probably hire people who actually build websites for a living.  Other than the fact that my devs were not actual web devs, though, there were no hiccups in the process that I feel I would need to account for in the future.
